What is M Directors?

M Directors is an online web-based navigation system that was created to improve the MMCM campus user experience by offering visitors, recently enrolled students, and regular students alike, quick, easy, and user-friendly navigation. M Directors guarantees easy navigation, saves time, and builds a sense of confidence and ease among the MMCM community with its user-friendly design, clear instructions, and support of campus exploration.

Our School

Welcome to Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao

Here in Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao, education meets excellence and relevance, everyone strives to be lifelong learners and responsible global citizens. With a learning environment that inspires curiosity, creativity and critical thinking we strive to empower one another and for our school, Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao to be a place where every student’s success is the ultimate priority.

Vision

Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao stands among the world's leading educational institutions.

Mission

The institute shall provide a learning environment in order for its students to acquire the attributes that will make them globally competitive & locally in-demand.
The institute shall engage in cutting edge and economically viable research, development and innovation that is relevant locally and beyond.
The institute shall provide state-of-the-art solutions to problems of industries and communities locally and beyond.

Motto

"Excellence and Relevance"
